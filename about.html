ABOUT US

Tools that respect your files.

Techsinghe Smart Tools started with one simple annoyance: why does editing a PDF mean uploading it to a stranger's server? So we built the opposite.

Our story

Most "free" PDF tools online quietly send your file to a server somewhere, process it, and send it back. That's fine for a random resume, but not for a signed contract, a payslip, or a client's tax return. We wanted a toolkit that never asks that trade-off of you in the first place.

Techsinghe Smart Tools is built entirely with in-browser JavaScript libraries — pdf-lib, pdf.js, JSZip, PapaParse, QRCode.js and heic2any. When you drop a file into any tool, it's read straight into your browser tab's memory, transformed there, and handed back to you as a download. Nothing crosses the network. Close the tab, and every trace of your file is gone.

What we believe

🔒

Privacy by default

Not a setting you have to enable — it's the only way this site works.

⚡

No waiting rooms

No upload progress bars, no server queues. Your own device does the work.

🆓

Free, genuinely

No paywalled "pro" tier hiding basic features. Every one of the 67 tools is free to use.

Who this is for

  • Students merging assignment scans or converting notes to PDF.
  • Freelancers & small businesses watermarking drafts, compressing invoices, or building contact sheets.
  • HR and legal teams Bates-numbering documents or stripping metadata before sharing.
  • Anyone who just wants a PDF or image task done without creating yet another account.

Where we're headed

We keep adding tools as long as they can run entirely client-side — that's the one rule we won't break. If there's a PDF or image task you wish existed here, tell us on the contact page.
