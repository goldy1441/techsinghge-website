Help Center

Quick answers to common questions about the toolkit and AI tools.

Last updated: 5 July 2026

Getting started

  • No account needed for the toolkit. Every PDF, image, and Excel/Office tool works instantly — just open a tool page and drop your file in.
  • AI tools need a free sign-in. Create a free account from the sign up page to start generating with any of the 45 AI tools.
  • Nothing is uploaded. The PDF/image/Excel toolkit processes your file entirely inside your browser tab — there's no server involved, so there's no upload wait and no file-size limit tied to a plan.

Common questions

A tool isn't working — what should I check first?

Make sure you're on an up-to-date version of Chrome, Edge, Firefox, or Safari. Try a smaller or simpler file first to rule out an unusual file structure, and check that JavaScript isn't blocked by a browser extension.

Why do the AI tools need sign-in but the PDF tools don't?

The PDF, image, and Excel tools run entirely on your device, so there's nothing to gate. The AI tools call a hosted AI model through a secure backend, so a free account lets us keep that usage fair and spam-free.

I forgot my password.

Use "Forgot password?" on the login page to get a reset link emailed to you.

Is my file or data ever stored on your servers?

No, for the PDF/image/Excel toolkit — see our Privacy Policy for full details. AI tool prompts are sent to a backend only to generate your result.

Still stuck?

Reach out via the contact page and we'll get back to you by email.

Get new tools in your inbox

One short email when we ship new AI or PDF/Office tools. No spam, unsubscribe anytime.
