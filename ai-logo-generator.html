How the AI Logo Generator works

Describe your brand, industry, style and colors, and this tool generates a clean, modern logo concept using simple bold shapes — ready to download and use as a starting point.

This tool calls an AI image model through a secure backend function, so the description you type here is sent to that backend to generate a response.
