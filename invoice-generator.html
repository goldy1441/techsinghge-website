What is the Invoice Generator tool?

Invoice Generator lets you fill in your business details, client details, and line items, then generates a polished, ready-to-send invoice PDF — all without any software to install or account to create. Everything is built locally using the pdf-lib library.

Freelancers and small businesses need to send invoices constantly, but not every job justifies paying for full invoicing software. This tool covers the essentials — itemized charges, totals, and your business details — and hands you a clean PDF in seconds.
