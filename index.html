FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Do I need to create an account to use the tools?

No. Every one of the 67 tools works with no sign-up. A free account unlocks saved history and all 45 AI tools.

Will the AI tools be free too?

A generous free tier is planned, with a Pro plan for higher usage. Pricing details are on the Pricing page.

Is any of my data sent to a server?

The 67 PDF, image and office tools run 100% in your browser — nothing is uploaded. The 45 AI tools are different: the text or description you submit is sent to our AI processing provider to generate a response. No files from the browser-based tools are ever sent anywhere.

What browsers are supported?

Any modern browser — Chrome, Edge, Firefox, or Safari, on desktop or mobile.
